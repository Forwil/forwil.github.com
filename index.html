Research

I'm interested in developing software/hardware system to support deeplearning. My work explores topics in HPC, compiler, runtime system and model quantization.

About me / bio

Fengwe Yu is a senior research at Sensetime Video Intellienge Research Team (STVIR) of Sensetime Research under the supervisor of Dr. Yan-Junjie , working on deep learning algorithm auto-deployment, model quantization and edge computing. He enjoy 2-years full-time internship in STVIR on developing high-performance video face/vehicle/pedestrian analysis system and porting algorithm into embedded system. Before join sensetime, He lead BUAA HPC team get first-prize on ASC(ASC Student Supercomputer Challenge) 2015 and spend half an year intership on Microsoft IOT team Beijing to develop R485 bus communication protocal. He received a Master and B.S. in computer science from the Beihang University under the supervision of xiaohua shi on JVM memory profiling/compcert/language model in 2018 and 2015.
